Curious scholar Gish pugilist

"There is so much information to discover and so many things to punch. Ah! This is fantastic!"

Yulia was born on the planet Orsapin to a human mother and an orcish father, in an integrated society that "respects racial differences". While cooperation between the indigenous races of Orsapin was celebrated, "half-breeds" were generally frowned upon and considered a dilution of the "great qualities" (read: stereotypical) of each race. Despite this, if you asked her, she’d say her childhood was grand. Between her mother and father, she learned both self-enlightenment and self-defense, fueling her lifelong hunger for both knowledge and victory.


Though, all good things must come to an end, and in the span of one week, both her parents’ lives did in her fourteenth year. Her even-tempered father was a bouncer at a local tavern, and upon ejecting some rather unsavory patrons, was stabbed literally in the back with a poisoned blade, before being thoroughly dispatched in an nearby alleyway. Yulia’s more hot-tempered mother swore vengeance, and received it, at the expense of her own life.


Yulia, an adult by orcish standards but not necessarily human ones, was adopted by her godmother Moira Pulver. Their relationship was a rather distant one, and while she’d known Moira her whole life, she’d never really seen her as a mother-figure, and Moira never asserted herself as one. After the Numarians discovered Orsapin and brought it into their empire, Yulia found that long-dormant hunger stir once more. She left her loose familial ties on Orsapin to get a position as a researcher on a Numarian Greenbird, eager to be the first Orsapian to see the universe.

Sin was born on the planet Orsapin to a human mother and an orcish father, in an integrated society that "respects racial differences".


While cooperation between the indigenous races of Orsapin was celebrated, "half-breeds" were generally frowned upon and considered a dilution of the "great qualities" (read: stereotypical) of each race.
